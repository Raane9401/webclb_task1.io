Paper Quilling

Quilling or paper filigree is an art form that involves the use of strips of paper that are rolled, shaped, and glued together to create decorative designs.Quilling starts with rolling a strip of paper into a coil and then pinching the coil into shapes that can be glued together.There are advanced techniques and different sized paper that are used to create 3D miniatures, abstract art and portraits among many things.Basic quilling techniques can be learned almost anywhere.Here u can find some paper quilling arts.

  • Photo Frame
  • Earrings
  • Greeting Cards
  • Quilling Baskets
  • Dolls
  • Wall hanging
  • Key Chains
