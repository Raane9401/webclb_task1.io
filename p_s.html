Pencil sketch

Pencil drawing is an essential first step for many artists and designers, but it can also produce stunning art and designs.A sketch may serve a number of purposes: it might record something that the artist sees, it might record or develop an idea for later use or it might be used as a quick way of graphically demonstrating an image, idea or principle.Sketches can be made in any drawing medium. The term is most often applied to graphic work executed in a dry medium such as silverpoint, graphite, pencil, charcoal or pastel.The latter two are generally referred to as "water colour sketches" and "oil sketches".Here u can find some pencil sketches.
