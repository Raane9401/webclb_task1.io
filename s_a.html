Stone Art

Stone Art specializes in natural stone applications and artifacts.Stone art is human-made markings placed on natural surfaces, typically vertical stone surfaces. rock art is found in many culturally diverse regions of the world.Anthropologists and archeologists define rock art as images carved, drawn, or painted onto immovable rock surfaces.Rock art is one of the oldest material forms of human expression and is found throughout the world.
